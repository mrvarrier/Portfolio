About me

I'm Manish, a UX Designer and Researcher who transforms complex technical challenges into elegant human experiences. My journey began in Computer Science and Engineering, where I developed a deep understanding of technology's inner workings. But I quickly realized my true passion lay not in the code itself, but in the human experience of interacting with it.

At Correlate AS, I've worn both Project Manager and UX Designer hats—giving me a rare 360° view of product development. This dual perspective allows me to speak the language of developers while advocating fiercely for users. My approach is methodical yet creative; I analyze problems systematically, but solve them with imagination, looking for the unexpected connections and hidden patterns in user behavior that others miss.

When I'm not designing, my camera becomes my third eye. Photography and travel aren't just hobbies—they're my training grounds for empathy and perspective. I bring this combination of technical knowledge, design thinking, and cultural curiosity to every project—creating digital experiences that don't just work flawlessly, but feel intuitively right.
