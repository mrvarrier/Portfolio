03. PROBLEM DEFINITION

The Remote Work Emotional Disconnect

The COVID-19 pandemic fundamentally transformed workplace communication, making remote interactions the standard. However, this shift revealed a critical gap: our natural ability to read emotional cues and build authentic relationships becomes severely limited through screens.
TARGET AUDIENCE
HR Professionals & Marketing Teams who rely on emotional intelligence for candidate assessment, team engagement evaluation, and strategic decision-making based on authentic human responses.
CORE CHALLENGE
Loss of Emotional Intelligence during video conferences directly impacts professionals' ability to build rapport, assess engagement, and make informed decisions about people and teams.
MARKET OPPORTUNITY
Seamless Integration Need for tools that enhance existing workflows rather than forcing users to adopt entirely new platforms and abandon familiar systems.
04. RESEARCH & DISCOVERY

Understanding Users and Technical Constraints

Desk Research & Market Analysis
Collaborated with our business analyst to investigate remote work preferences, video platform success factors, and competitive landscape. We analyzed why certain platforms succeed while others fail, identifying key opportunities for our emotional analytics solution.
User Interviews with Video Platform Users
Conducted interviews with 8 regular users of Google Meet and Zoom to understand their experiences, pain points, and workflow preferences. This research informed our decision to design for familiarity rather than forcing users to learn new interaction patterns.
Professional Role-Based Research
Interviewed 12 professionals across HR and marketing roles from different companies to identify specific use cases, desired insights, and integration requirements. This research directly shaped our target audience definition and feature prioritization.
INSIGHT 01
Simplicity vs. Features Balance: Users preferred Zoom's rich features but Google Meet's simple flow. They wanted comprehensive functionality without interface complexity that slows down their primary goal of effective communication.
INSIGHT 02
Time Constraints Matter: Professionals with back-to-back meetings need immediate insights. Detailed analytics are valuable, but quick emotional summaries are essential for busy schedules.
INSIGHT 03
Professional Context Specificity: HR professionals need different emotional indicators than marketing teams. Hiring managers focus on candidate comfort and authenticity, while marketing teams prioritize engagement and reaction patterns.
05. TECHNICAL CHALLENGES

ML Model Training & Platform Integration

🧠
Machine Learning Model Optimization
Training an accurate emotion detection model within 36 hours while ensuring real-time performance (60 FPS analysis) presented significant technical constraints. We needed to balance model accuracy with processing speed and resource efficiency.
SOLUTION APPROACH
I collaborated directly with our development team to optimize the ML model, contributing to training data curation and testing different model architectures. We implemented a lightweight model that could run efficiently in browsers while maintaining acceptable accuracy for our use case.
📊
Data Visualization Complexity
The biggest design challenge was presenting complex emotional data in an accessible, actionable format. We needed visualizations that anyone could understand regardless of data literacy, while ensuring the insights were comprehensive enough for professional decision-making.
SOLUTION APPROACH
I designed a multi-layered dashboard approach: immediate pie chart summaries for quick insights, detailed timeline graphs for pattern analysis, and contextual highlights linking emotional peaks to conversation transcripts.

Problem Statement

"How might we help marketing and HR professionals better understand and respond to the emotional dynamics of their virtual meetings while maintaining familiar workflows and providing actionable insights that enhance their professional effectiveness?"
